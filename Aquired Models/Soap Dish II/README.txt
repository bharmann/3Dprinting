# Summary

The wife wanted a soap dish that would clip on to our vertical shower caddy.

I took the dish part from https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:402087 and made it bigger along the Y-axis.
I cut the clip part from https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:754176 and made the gap smaller.
I then added the clip to the dish.
